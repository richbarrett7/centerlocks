User Guidance

Preparation

Work in a well-ventilated area. Wear old clothes - the paint may stain permanently.

Ensure the wheels are cool to the touch and clean. Use the alcohol wipe provided to clean all surfaces before applying decals or paint.

Masking & Painting

Use the spline masks to protect the wheel while spraying the toothed part of the centerlock nut.

The rubberised paint contains synthetic rubber, organic solvents (such as toluene, xylene, and n-hexane), plasticizers, resins, stabilizers, and pigments. These ingredients provide flexibility, adhesion, and color but may be flammable and harmful if inhaled. Use in a well-ventilated area and avoid prolonged exposure

Cover adjacent bodywork and wheels to avoid overspray. Allow at least 24 hours for the paint to fully cure before handling or driving.

Decal Application

Ensure surfaces are completely dry.

The decals are heavy duty 12-mil Polyvinyl Chloride with a Glossy UV PET for added durability and weather resistance. They are designed for used in harsh conditions. Apply with care to avoid lifting or misalignment.
